PASSION

For many decades we have been in the hairdresser business providing beauty and hair care services and thanks to professionals like you, we continue to work with enthusiasm for the beauty of today and tomorrow!
Our electrical appliances & hair care range are especially developed to enable you to unfold your creativity.

INNOVATION

Our research laboratories are constantly developing new formulas to render every product more effective and easier to use.

The BaByliss PRO electrical appliances range is designed for hairstylists and covers all product categories (hairdryers, straighteners, curling irons, setters, clippers & trimmers etc.)

Our hair care range,  rich in essential oils and plant extracts, consists of shampoos, conditioners, hairstyle and technical products, which are all perfectly adapted to the use with our professional electrical appliances, for an all around hair care.

REFERENCE

Every electrical appliance has been previously tested in our technical centres around the world, and combine power, design, ergonomics and performance. This is why BaByliss PRO is until today the brand reference in the hairdresser market.

INSPIRATION

Every day we follow our aim to anticipate your needs of tomorrow, in order to be able to add a limitless, artistic dimension to the hairdresser profession. Our product’s performance and quality are rigorously designed to match your talent.

Today, we are the unique undisputed actors in hair care in France and propose hair specialists a global service covering all products from electrical appliance to hair care products.

Our formulas and instruments are perfectly adapted to the moves and high technology standards of professionals and dedicated to the beauty of your hair!

RESEARCH & DEVELOPMENT

The know-how of the R&D engineers is at your disposal in order to develop products at the best price, which correspond to your market needs. The skills and experience of the manufacturing specialists guarantee a high-quality production.
